            To Install Gmail on your computer

            1. Visit this Gmail download page in any browser, such as Microsoft Edge.
            2. Click the . The Gmail Installer will offer the best version. Click “No thank” if it's not for a child.
            3. Click Yes, continue if you are a child.
            4. Complete your name and click Next.
            5. Enter birthday and gender. Then click Next.
            6. Choose or create a Gmail address. Click Next.
            7. Create a password.
            8. Verify your phone number for security.
            9. Click Gmail and save your account in your browser.
            10. Finally, your Gmail account is set up.
